How Our Solution Empowers Genuine Healthcare

            
    
                
  • 
                    Unbreakable Medicine Identity: Every medicine strip or bottle receives a tamper-proof, blockchain-based unique 
                    QR/NFC tag at the point of manufacture, making forgery virtually impossible and giving each unit an immutable digital identity 
                    that can be verified anywhere.
    • 
                
  • End-to-End Traceability: Custody transfers - from manufacturer to distributor, to retailer - are securely 
                    recorded as signed blockchain transactions, establishing a freely auditable supply trail that exposes fakes and unauthorized 
                    handling.
                
    • 
                
  • Instant Authenticity Checks: Scanning the QR/NFC code instantly reveals the full supply chain, validates each 
                    link via cryptographic hash checks, and enables quick detection of tampering, counterfeits, and expired medicines at any 
                    point - from rural clinics to urban pharmacies.
    • 
                
  • Production-Grade Security: The system incorporates robust server-side blockchain infrastructure, secure QR/NFC 
                    tag generation, advanced key management, KYC verification for all supply actors, and dynamic liveness checks for on-phone 
                    scanning - making every point in the chain resilient to fraud and system abuse.
